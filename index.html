ABOUT US

Engaged in the midstream and downstream sectors and is owned by PT Perusahaan Gas Negara Tbk and

PT Pertamina (Persero), Pertamina Gas is a part of Indonesia's gas subholding.

Gas Transportation

Gas transportation activities carried out through the transmission pipeline originate from the shipper in accordance with the gas transportation agreement. Shippers are in the form of contractor companies, power plants to industry.

Niaga Gas

Niaga gas Pertamina Gas and its subsidiaries serve gas sales intended for industry, households, KKKS (Cooperation Contract Contractors), and other commercials (office centers to HOREKA).

Gas Processin

Gas processing activities in terms of Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) products take place in several third-party refinery facilities in collaboration with Pertamina Gas and its subsidiaries.

Operating Area

Several regions, from industrial to commercial segments, have trusted Pertamina Gas as Indonesia's gas holding company.
